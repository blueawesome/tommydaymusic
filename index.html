About

I have 20 years of experience playing gigs all over Indiana—bars, restaurants, coffee shops, wineries, weddings, festivals, and just about everything in between. No matter the venue, I’ve always aimed to create the right vibe, whether that means getting a crowd singing along or just setting the perfect background mood.

My setlists are shaped by the music I grew up with—the songs that played in the backseat of my parents’ car and echoed through the house. Late-night soft rock on Delilah After Dark, classic rock blasting from my dad’s boombox, old Patsy Cline records, and the pop hits of the ‘80s and ‘90s. That mix of influences is a big part of what I play live, and it carries into my original music too. I write and record under the name Secret Oats, with new music coming soon.

By day I'm a freelance web developer, and in my dreams, I'm directing movies about UFOs and dinosaurs.
